Passport Pals is a **prototype web application** designed to reimagine travel as a form of **cultural exchange**, rather than a commercial transaction. Instead of hotels or generic tourist experiences, Passport Pals connects like-minded travelers who **host and get hosted** in each otherâ€™s homes while exploring new countries through real local perspectives.

The concept is inspired by student exchange programs and modern swipe-based apps, combining **interest and personality matching** with a community-driven approach to travel. The focus is on meaningful connections, shared experiences, and discovering a place through the people who live there.

**Travel through people, not places. Real cultural exchange, not check-ins. Because the best trips aren't about rooms, they're about people.**
